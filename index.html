Our Mission

EconEd is a student-led nonprofit dedicated to making financial literacy accessible and engaging for young students. By teaching the fundamentals of budgeting, saving, and responsible spending early on, we empower the next generation to build strong financial habits before poor ones take in place.

Student Community

Building a supportive community where students can learn and grow together through shared economic experiences.

Economic Education

Providing practical, hands-on economic workshops tailored to students' needs and learning styles.

Accessibility

Making economic literacy accessible through local student-led chapters across the country.
