Our Story

EconEd was founded with a mission to make economic education accessible to all students, regardless of background or resources. Our journey began with a simple idea: to empower the next generation with financial literacy and economic understanding.

Foundation Image

Our Foundation

Started by passionate students who recognized the gap in economic education in schools, EconEd began as a small tutoring initiative that quickly grew into something much larger.

Growth Image

Our Growth

From a single chapter to multiple locations across the country, our organization has expanded its reach while maintaining its core mission of accessible economic education.

Impact Image

Our Impact

Today, EconEd has helped thousands of students develop financial literacy skills, preparing them for future success in both personal finance and potential careers in economics.
