Our Mission

EconEd is a student-led nonprofit dedicated to making financial literacy accessible and engaging for young students, particularly those in marginalized communities, including Hispanic and Black communities that have historically been excluded from quality financial education. By teaching the fundamentals of budgeting, saving, and responsible spending early on, we aim to close opportunity gaps and empower the next generation to build strong financial habits before systemic barriers and harmful financial practices take hold.

Student Community

Building a supportive community where students can learn and grow together through shared economic experiences.

Economic Education

Providing practical, hands-on economic workshops tailored to students' needs and learning styles.

Accessibility

Making economic literacy accessible through local student-led chapters across the country.
