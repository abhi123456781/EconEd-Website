Create an EconEd Chapter

Bring EconEd to your community and help empower students with economic literacy and financial education skills.

Bring Financial Literacy to Your School or Community

Want to lead a movement at your own school? Start an EconEd chapter and be the reason students in your area learn real-world money skills early.

You'll organize outreach efforts, lead workshops, and help younger students build confidence with budgeting, saving, and spending.

We make it easy to get started. Once you have a team and a school in mind, we'll guide you through the rest with lesson plans, training, and ongoing support.

Steps to Start a Chapter

1

Build Your Team

Gather a team of 3 to 5 dedicated students who share your passion for financial literacy.

2

Reach Out to Schools

Connect with a local elementary or middle school that would benefit from your program.

3

Submit Interest Form

Fill out our Chapter Interest Form to officially begin the process of starting your chapter.

4

Meet With Our Team

Set up a quick meeting with our team to discuss your goals and answer any questions.

5

Get Your Chapter Kit

Receive your EconEd Chapter Kit and start planning your first event with our resources.

Ready to begin? Fill out this form and we'll reach out to schedule your onboarding meeting.
