About EconEd

We're on a mission to make economic education accessible to all students.

Our Story

EconEd was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the digital divide for students everywhere.

What started as a small group of volunteers has grown into a nationwide movement with chapters across the country.

Today, EconEd continues to expand its reach, empowering students with the tools they need to navigate the economy.
