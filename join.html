Join EconEd

Become part of our mission to empower students with economic literacy and financial education skills.

Teach Kids What Schools Don't

Most students grow up learning math and science, but never how to budget, save, or spend wisely. At EconEd, we're changing that by bringing financial literacy into classrooms early, when it matters most. We teach simple, practical money skills to kids in a way that's fun, clear, and easy to remember.

If you're passionate about education, finance, or just want to make a real difference, EconEd is the place to start. You don't need to be an expert. What matters most is that you care. Join us in giving the next generation the tools they need to take control of their future.

No Chapter Near You?

Don't see an EconEd chapter in your area? Consider starting one! We provide all the resources and support you need to bring economic education to students in your community.
